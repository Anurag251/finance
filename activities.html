Insurance agent agreement between our Sagarmatha Multipurpose Cooperative Society Limited and Prabhu Lai Insurance

Shravan 28. Kathmandu. Our Sagarmatha Multipurpose Cooperative Society Limited has got Prabhu to act as the insurance agent of the insurance company. The agreement was signed by KB, Chief Executive Officer of Prabhu Life Insurance and Chairman of our Sagarmatha Multipurpose Cooperative. Upreti signed the agreement.
